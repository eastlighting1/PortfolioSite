During a practical project I worked on over the previous vacation, one of my responsibilities involved planning, designing, building, and maintaining tools for internal team use. Only later did I learn that this kind of work is often called platform engineering. Since it was the kind of task that kept me interested the entire time, I started to wonder whether it might be the kind of role I could realistically see myself continuing in the future, and how I might build deeper professional strength around it as part of the skills that represent me.

While thinking about that, I came across a blog post worth saving for reference:
