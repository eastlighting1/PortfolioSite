It felt like the attendance certificate arrived almost twenty days later.

The sessions I listened to at the time included Yoon Seokchan’s opening recap of AWS re:Invent, Chae Jeonghui’s talk on design principles using the AWS Well-Architected Framework, Jo Sohyun’s session on VPC and on-premises network connectivity, Kim Jeongwon’s session on cost management through Cost Explorer, and Jeong Jaemin’s session on monitoring with CloudWatch. There were many other talks I also wanted to hear, but because I had other commitments afterward, I ended up focusing only on the ones that seemed most immediately useful to the project I was working on at the time.

Thankfully, AWS Builders Online Series makes the sessions available afterward, so I was glad to know I could come back and watch more once the project settled down. I was especially interested in future talks on serverless, data governance, Amazon Bedrock, and CDK, since all of those feel directly connected to the skills I want to strengthen in cloud, data, and MLOps-oriented work.

I hope 2024 becomes a year full of cloud and data.
