## Summary

Refreshing a clinical risk model requires more than comparing aggregate validation scores. This engineering note documents the monitoring and review artifacts needed to support safe and understandable refresh decisions.

## Key Points

- Cohort checks should be reviewed alongside model metrics.
- Calibration and threshold changes need reviewer-facing context.
- Artifact lineage matters when preparing evidence for internal review.

## Practical Guidance

The note provides a compact checklist for teams moving from research workflows to scheduled, auditable model operations.
