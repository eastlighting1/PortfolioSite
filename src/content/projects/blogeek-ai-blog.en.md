## Context

BloGeek was developed as part of the Kakao Enterprise program and gave the team a chance to work on a blog-oriented AI project with multiple NLP components.

## Problem

Making generated content feel expressive and personalized requires more than plain generation quality. The system also needs signals about tone, emotional context, and stylistic control.

## Implementation

My contribution focused on the machine learning side of the project, particularly around emotion recognition, style transfer, and supporting data work such as augmentation. Because the project involved multiple contributors, collaboration and clear division of responsibilities were important parts of execution.

## Outcome

BloGeek added collaborative product-building experience to my NLP work and reinforced the value of combining classification-oriented and generation-oriented components in one user-facing project.
